The Studio

A photography & film practice covering weddings, events, commercial, fashion and family work — run with one person's eye and one clear standard.

The Person Behind It

Hi, I'm Adarsh Singhal

Founder - Adario The Studio

I started this studio because I'd rather be the person quietly working the room with a camera than the one being photographed — and because every wedding, shoot or event has a handful of moments that are easy to miss if you're not paying close attention. That's the job: pay attention, stay ready, and hand back a set of photos and films that actually feel like the day did.

Adario The Studio now covers weddings, events, commercial work, fashion editorials and family sessions, all shot and edited with the same care.

How I Shoot

Three things that don't change

Real over staged

Direction when it helps, invisibility when it doesn't. Most of the best frames happen when no one's posing.

One point of contact

You talk to the person actually shooting your day, from the first message to the final delivery.

No bloated packages

You get what you need for your day — not a tier sheet designed to upsell you.
